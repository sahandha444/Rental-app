1. Owner selects a car from the dashboard page and press new rental.

2. App shows a QR code + a link to the agreement website. 

3. Customer scans the QR code or clicks the shared link on his/her phone.

4. After agreeing to the conditions, it forwards to the form website.

5. Owner also get forwarded into the form page. 

6. Here, Owner and customer both can fill the name, NIC no, address 
and phone number of the customer. Also it can be signed by both phones. Only owner can upload photos to the form. Owner should upload 1-5 car photos(optional) and a meter mileage photo(necessary).

7. After the customer finished the submission he should wait for the confirmation of the owner. 

8. After the confirmation of the customer he/she should be forwarded to a page where he can download the photos the owner uploaded, the agreement and also the invoice.

9. The rental should be saved to the rental history of the owner with the invoice.
